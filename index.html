Custom Bank Payment
Gateway Integration

Client: Georgian (Country) eCommerce Merchant

The Challenge

A merchant in Georgia (the country) had a major roadblock — no ready-made Shopify integrations for local banks. They needed customers to pay via local credit cards AND access monthly installment loans at checkout.

The Solution

  • ✅ Custom payment app using Shopify's Payments SDK
  • ✅ Secure connection to Bank of Georgia API
  • ✅ "Buy Now, Pay Later" flow with bank loan redirects
  • ✅ Webhooks for automatic payment confirmation
  • ✅ OAuth 2.0 and JWT encryption — 100% secure transactions
  • ✅ Installment button styled natively into checkout

The Result

The store can now accept local payments and offer installments — previously impossible on Shopify in that region. Opened their business to thousands of new customers who prefer to pay monthly.
